Among Us is a social deduction game that challenges players to work together—or secretly sabotage their team—on a spaceship.

Roblox is an online platform where users create, share, and play games using Roblox Studio, interact with others, and use Robux to customize their experience.

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game where players compete in large-scale battles across dynamic maps like Verdansk and Rebirth Island. Featuring intense firefights, tactical gameplay, and modes like Resurgence and Strongholds, Warzone offers fast-paced action with cross-platform play. The game includes frequent updates, new weapons, and seasonal events, keeping the experience fresh for both casual and competitive players. With squad-based strategies, unique contracts, and evolving gameplay mechanics, it remains one of the most engaging multiplayer shooters.

Fall Guys is a lively and chaotic multiplayer game where up to 32 players compete in colorful obstacle courses and mini-games, aiming to survive each round and claim the ultimate crown. Developed by Mediatonic and later bought by epic games, the game features jellybean-shaped characters and blends whimsical visuals with fast-paced gameplay, offering a mix of humor and challenge. Its customisation options, regular updates, and playful yet competitive environment make it a favorite among casual and competitive gamers alike.

Fortnite is a globally popular battle royale game developed by Epic Games, featuring fast-paced gameplay, creative building mechanics, and frequent updates that keep players engaged.
